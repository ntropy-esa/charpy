Mission

Bring biochar products to the Lebanese agriculture & industries, while valorising local biomass resources, striving for efficient water & nutrient use, and with climate change mitigation and adaptation as side effect.

About Us

Charpy started in 2021 in the most severe economic crisis that Lebanon is going through. This crisis was a fatal blow to the agricultural and industrial sector. For this reason, we took it upon ourselves to put the expertise of our team to support the agricultural and industrial sector through sustainable and environmental technology.

Charpy aims to offer environmental and more affordable products, contributing to solving farmer liquidity issues, high energy prices, and waste management challenges, while mitigating and adapting to climate change in Lebanon
